# Twitter Tasks User Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you understand how to use Twitter tasks and automatic verification.

## What are Twitter Tasks?

Twitter tasks are activities you can complete on Twitter to earn points. There are three types:

1. **Follow Tasks** - Follow a specific Twitter account
2. **Like Tasks** - Like a specific tweet
3. **Retweet Tasks** - Retweet or quote tweet a specific tweet

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Connect Your Twitter Account

Before you can complete Twitter tasks, you need to connect your Twitter account:

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Find the "Twitter Connection" section
3. Click **"Connect Twitter"**
4. You'll be redirected to Twitter
5. Click **"Authorize app"** on Twitter
6. You'll be redirected back to your profile

‚úÖ **Done!** Your Twitter account is now connected.

1. Click on a Twitter task to open the details

5. Return to the task page

## Automatic Verification

When your Twitter account is connected, verification happens automatically!

### How It Works

1. You complete a Twitter task
2. Our system checks your Twitter activity
3. If verified, you instantly receive your points
4. You'll see a success message

## Without Twitter Connection

You can still complete Twitter tasks without connecting your account:

1. Click on the task
2. Click **"Manual Mode"**
3. Complete the action on Twitter
4. Your submission will be reviewed manually by admins
5. Points will be awarded after approval (may take longer)

## Troubleshooting

### "Twitter Connection Required" Message

**Problem**: You see a message asking you to connect Twitter.

**Solution**: 
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Connect your Twitter account
3. Return to the task and try again

### "Token Expired" Message

**Problem**: Your Twitter connection has expired.

**Solution**:
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click **"Reconnect"** in the Twitter Connection section
3. Authorize the app again on Twitter

### "Verification Failed" Message

**Problem**: The system couldn't verify your action.

**Solutions**:
- Make sure you completed the action on Twitter
- Wait a few seconds and click **"Retry Verification"**
- Check that you're logged into the correct Twitter account
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting your Twitter account

### Task Shows "Rejected"

**Problem**: Your task was rejected after verification.

**Possible Reasons**:
- You didn't complete the action (follow/like/retweet)
- You completed the action but then undid it
- You're using a different Twitter account

**Solution**:
1. Complete the action on Twitter again
2. Click **"Retry Verification"**
3. Make sure not to undo the action

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is it safe to connect my Twitter account?

**A**: Yes! We use Twitter's official OAuth 2.0 system. We can only read your public information and verify your actions. We cannot post or make changes to your account.

### Q: How long does verification take?

**A**: With automatic verification, it's instant (usually 1-3 seconds). Manual verification can take up to 24 hours.

### Q: Can I disconnect and reconnect later?

**A**: Yes! You can disconnect and reconnect anytime. Your points and task history are preserved.

### Q: What if I accidentally unfollowed after verification?

**A**: The task will remain completed. However, we encourage you to keep following accounts you've followed through tasks to support the community.

### Q: Can I complete tasks on mobile?

**A**: Yes! The Twitter connection and verification work on both desktop and mobile devices.

### Q: Do I need to keep the app open during verification?

**A**: No! Verification happens in the background. You can close the app and check back later.
